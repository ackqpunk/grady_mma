TRAIN IN A FAMILY ENVIRONMENT

A Family Orientated School

Grady's Family Mixed Martial Arts in Clearwater, Florida is a family oriented and structured Martial Arts school. You have the option to not participate in any exercise that you don't feel comfortable with yet, and you will not be looked down upon for not participating. We teach Marital Arts to everyone ages 7 and older, but all of ours Kids Mixed Martial Arts classes are taught separately to focus on safety. Extra precautions are taken while teaching children, and they are only taught basics with no chance of injury to themselves or their classmates.
